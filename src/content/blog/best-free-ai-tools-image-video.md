# Best Free AI Tools for Image and Video Editing

AI-powered image and video editing tools have revolutionized the creative industry, making professional-grade editing accessible to everyone. Here's our curated list of the best free AI tools for image and video editing.

## Image Editing AI Tools

### 1. Canva AI Image Generator
Canva's AI image generator allows users to create stunning visuals with simple text prompts. Perfect for social media content and marketing materials.

**Key Features:**
- Text-to-image generation
- Background removal
- Smart resizing
- Template suggestions

### 2. Remove.bg
A powerful tool for automatically removing backgrounds from images. Simply upload your image and let AI handle the rest.

**Key Features:**
- Automatic background removal
- Batch processing
- API availability
- High-quality output

### 3. Deep Dream Generator
Transform your photos into artistic masterpieces using neural network-based style transfer.

**Key Features:**
- Multiple art styles
- Customizable parameters
- High-resolution exports

## Video Editing AI Tools

### 4. Runway
An AI-powered video editing platform with impressive capabilities for creators.

**Key Features:**
- Video generation from text
- Style transfer
- Background removal
- Motion tracking

### 5. Pictory
Transform long-form content into engaging short videos automatically.

**Key Features:**
- Auto-summarization
- Text-to-video
- Caption generation
- Brand customization

### 6. Descript
An all-in-one video editing tool with powerful AI features.

**Key Features:**
- AI transcription
- Screen recording
- Podcast editing
- Voice cloning

## How to Choose the Right Tool

Consider these factors when selecting an AI editing tool:

1. **Your Skill Level**: Some tools are more beginner-friendly than others
2. **Output Quality**: Check sample outputs before committing
3. **File Formats**: Ensure compatibility with your workflow
4. **Export Options**: Look for high-quality export settings

## Conclusion

AI tools for image and video editing have democratized creative content creation. Whether you're a social media manager, content creator, or hobbyist, these free tools can significantly enhance your workflow and output quality.
